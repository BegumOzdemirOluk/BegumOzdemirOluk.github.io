I am a doctoral candidate at the University of St. Gallen (HSG). My PhD is funded by the Swiss Federal Institute of Aquatic Science and Technology (Eawag). I am affiliated with the Swiss Institute for International Economics and Applied Economic Research (SIAW) at HSG. I hold a B.Sc. in Management (Finance specialization) and an M.A. in Economics from Bilkent University, Turkey, as well as an M.S. in Economics and a minor in Mathematics from the University of North Texas, United States.
